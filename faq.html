Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DSCE clubs and events

What is DSCE Clubs?

DSCE Clubs is a centralized platform that allows students to explore, join, and participate in technical, cultural, and non-technical clubs.

How do I register for an event?

Navigate to the Events section, choose an event, and complete the registration form provided.

Is there any registration fee?

Most events are free. Some workshops or competitions may have a small registration fee, which will be clearly mentioned.

Can I join multiple clubs?

Yes, students can join multiple clubs as long as the event schedules do not clash.

Who can participate in club events?

All DSCE students are eligible to participate unless stated otherwise by the organizing club.

How will I receive event updates?

Event updates are shared through the dashboard, official announcements, and club notifications.
