Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions related to college events and registrations.

What is the College Event Management System?

It helps students view, register, and participate in college events like workshops and seminars.

How can I register for an event?

You can register by visiting the Events page and filling out the registration form.

Is there any registration fee?

Some events are free while others may require a registration fee.

Can I attend multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events if the timings do not clash.
